About

Hello, my name is Aleah. Fun fact about me: I did not get my start in web design to begin with. I actually began my adult life studying anthropology and archaeology. I chose these fields because I have always loved helping people to tell their stories. Sometimes those stories are told through stories of cultures told around the fire, or through artifacts found on a digsite, and sometimes those stories are told through the web. I believe that storytelling is, was, and always will be fundamental to human connections, but now in the digital age, we’re more likely to be telling stories around the warmth of a computer instead of the warmth of a fire.
